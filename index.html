About Me

Hello, thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. I am a fullstack software engineer with a strong passion for STEM related fields. This has provided me with a unique background in winemaking, healthcare and now software development! After completing my bachelors of science at UC Davis, I spent time in the hills of Paso Robles producing fine wine with California’s best dry farmed Zinfandel. Following that pursuit, I obtained my EMT certification and spent time in the back of an ambulance serving my local community. My latest test of knowledge has been completing App Academy's Full Stack software engineering program. Over the course of the bootcamp, I developed solid programming foundations in both Javascript and Python. I developed a preference for working with backend code, specifically utilizing both the Express and Flask frameworks in my work. I look forward to entering the tech industry and further enhancing the skills I developed in the program. My knowledge is strong in technologies listed below.
